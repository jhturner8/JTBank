Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our banking services and products.

Account & Banking

Our branches are open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Branches are closed on Weekends and public holidays.

You can open an account by visiting your nearest branch with a valid ID and proof of address.

Here at JT Bank, we offer various savings accounts, including basic savings, high-yield savings, and accounts tailored for students and senior citizens.

Loans & Mortgages

Here at JT Bank we offer a variety of loans such as personal loans, business loans, auto loans, and mortgages with flexible repayment terms and competitive interest rates.

You can apply for a loan by visiting a branch, or by contacting our customer service team for assistance.

Mortgage eligibility depends on factors such as income, credit score, and existing debts. Use our Mortgage Calculator to estimate your loan affordability.

Cards & Payments

You can apply for a credit card online or at any of our branches. Choose from a range of cards designed for rewards, cashback, or low-interest rates.

If you lose your card, immediately contact our 24/7 helpline at 0800-123-4567 to block your card. You can request a replacement card through online banking or at any branch.

Foreign transaction fees vary by card type. Premium accounts often include reduced or waived foreign transaction fees. Check your card's terms and conditions for specific details.
